Arab Pharmaceutical Glass Company

Over 25 years of experience in the glass production field has earned APG an immense understanding of customers needs. This has led to greater flexibility in producing standardized & customized products. Its range of standard and custom products covers ; Ogivals, Verals, Vials, Drops, Jars, Other Syrup Bottles, Juice bottles and Foodstuff jars.

Since its establishment in 1980’s, Arab Pharmaceutical Glass Company S.A.E. (APG) has been recognized as a pioneer manufacturer of pharmaceutical glass products in the Middle East region.

APG S.A.E was formed as an independent subsidiary of ACDIMA (Arab Company for Drug Industries & Medical Appliances) with 78% of the shares held by ACDIMA and 22% held by National Bank of Egypt and Egyptian Holding Pharmaceutical Company.

With a compounded annual sales growth of 10% for the entire pharma flint and amber bottles & vials, APG enjoys its leadership position in the niche pharmaceutical packaging markets, as it is the only company in the middle east and one of the few in the world who manufacture and market the entire pharma range of glass bottles and vials.
