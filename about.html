The Story of success

APG has invested extensively in modern manufacturing tools & production infrastructure to meet and exceed the needs of its quality conscious customers.

APG modem facilities include electronically controlled batch house, glass melting furnaces, 6 automated production lines, electronically controlled state- of-the-art forming machines and inspection machines.

Using the latest technology that is being handled with professional staff, APG is able to produce 1.25 million glass bottles and vials every day throughout the year.

APG main strategy was built on customers’ needs and expectations, through which the company was able to achieve sustainable development and strong market presence.

APG Total Quality Management (TQM) APG demonstrates its commitment to quality through;Documentation and data control. Traceability systems. State of art metrology labs. Continuous training of the staff.

APG Vision Our vision to expand our regional leadership position in the pharmaceutical glass manufacturing field.

APG Mission Our mission is to work for the success of our customers by providing reliable & high quality glass products that best satisfy their needs. We deeply believe that our customers success is ours.

APG is the largest manufacturer of pharmaceutical glass in the middle east Through its advanced manufacturing technology, superior technical expertise, APG’s reliable quality and perfect services (focusing on aftersale services), has gained the domestic and international customer’s extensive trust. Its products are sold to more than 100 local and regional pharmaceutical companies
